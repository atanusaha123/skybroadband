Bandwidth for Flexibility: Make your own plan

Indinet offers you the ultimate flexibility to Make Your Own Plan (MYOP). When subscribing to a Indinet Broadband connection, MYOP is a simple two step decision – Step 1: Select one of the 5 speeds offered – 10 Mbps, 15 Mbps, 20 Mbps, 50 Mbps, 100 Mbps Step 2: Select one of the 6 Data Usage Limits available – 50 GB, 65 GB, 85 GB, 115 GB, 160 GB, 225 GB. Enjoy the Indinet experience..
